Plasma Donation

Plasma therapy is a technique in which patients who have recovered from covid-19 donate their plasma. That plasma contains antibodies which help other patients fight the infection.

If you have recovered from coronavirus, you can save lives by donating your plasma.

How it Works?

  • Your privacy is our biggest concern and therefore, your provided data won’t be public. Our system will find a match for you and we will connect you both through Whatsapp.
  • The process will be quick and efficient, but since it depends on two sides, it can take anywhere from 2 hours to 2 days.
